Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to email inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Phone and live chat support provide immediate assistance during our support hours.

Can you help me with my N-400 application?

While we provide educational guidance on the naturalization process, we cannot provide legal advice. For help with your N-400 application, we recommend consulting with a qualified immigration attorney.

Do you offer one-on-one tutoring sessions?

Yes! We offer personalized tutoring sessions with our expert instructors. Contact us to learn more about scheduling and pricing for individual sessions.

What if I don't pass my citizenship test?

Don't worry! USCIS allows you to retake the test. We provide additional support and targeted study materials to help you prepare for your retake at no extra cost.

Can I access the course materials on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and computers. You can study anywhere, anytime.

How often is your content updated?

We review and update our content monthly to ensure it reflects the latest USCIS requirements and test changes. You'll always have access to the most current information.
